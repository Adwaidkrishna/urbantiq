The Journey

Our Story

Founded in 2024, URBANTIQ was born out of a simple observation: modern men want quality clothing that doesn't scream for attention. We set out to create a wardrobe that balances architectural precision with everyday comfort.

Every stitch, every fabric choice, and every silhouette is carefully considered. We blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design to deliver pieces that feel as good as they look.

Our mission is to empower the modern generation to express their identity through a lens of minimal luxury and sustainable practices.
